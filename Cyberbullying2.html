How does 'flaming' start?


  • Flaming and cyberbullying often begin in online interactions due to conflicts, disagreements, or provocations. On anonymous platforms, individuals may feel emboldened to engage in aggressive behavior.

  • Misunderstandings, personal attacks can contribute to the escalation. The lack of empathy can amplify the negative impact. We should work towards fostering a positive and supportive online environment through digital literacy and promoting respectful behavior.
