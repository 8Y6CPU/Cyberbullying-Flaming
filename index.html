What is flaming?


Flaming

Internet

Flaming is the act of posting insults, usually including profanity and other offensive language. Multiple individuals who participate in this will be considered a 'Flame War' which may cause servere long-lasting mental damage to both individuals and communities.

Where are they found: Social Media

Social media has given us the ability to spread information across the world, however it is a place that festers toxicity and encourages 'Flame wars' by anonymity of their users giving them immunity from consequences if it wasn't present.

Where are they found: Video Games

Video games help to promote cooperation and teamwork in exchange for rewards. However, some have taken is further than others, only seeing complete victory as the only viable option. This has caused them to be furious as their teammates or allies for reasons they see fit.

Examples of flaming in real life



In Social Media

One such example of a flame war on social media is the war between American Democrats and Republicans on platforms such as X (Formally known as twitter) garnering notoriety for being so bad that it has even resulted in injuries and even fatalities.


These are routinely cited as an example for how social media as a whole is harmful to society due to how aggressive the flame wars that arise from disagreements on social media with even political figures such as Donald Trump, SCOTUS (Supreme Court Of The United States) regularly debate about their place in today's world of information.



In Video Games

Another example is in video games where flame-wars have frequently started over peformance of teammates or even over-peformance of enemies.


This can be seen in the video game 'League Of Legends' where it has been rated to be one of the most toxic games due to their players behaviours and mindset about the game. They regularly view victory as the only option, if not, they'll result into bashing their teammates and enemies for the outcome.
