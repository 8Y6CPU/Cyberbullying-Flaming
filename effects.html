Effects of Flaming

Effects: There are different impacts on certain groups of people. For adults, it is usually minimal and they see as a joke rather than an actual reason to be enraged by However, for youths/children, they have not fully developed their brain and thus are vunerable to these type of comments online. (Why?) As it is due to their inability to cope or deal with these comments due to their age. (What will happen?) They will eventually become absorbed by the argument due to their inability to cope and move on from it, this lingering effect may cause the victim to be mentally harmed. (Results) Decreased grades, isolation from peers and family, refusal to follow their daily routine such as going to school, and if servere, suicide.
