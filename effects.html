Effects of Flaming

Adults

For adults, it is usually minimal and they see it as a joke rather than an actual thing to be enraged by.

Children

However, for youths/children, they have not fully developed their brains and thus are vulnerable to these types of comments online.

This is due to their inability to deal with these comments.

This might lead to decreased grades, isolation from peers and family, refusal to go to school, and if severe, suicide.
